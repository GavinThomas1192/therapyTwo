Out-of-network benefits: Contact your insurance company and ask how much they'll reimburse for therapy provided by an out-of-network LICSW-A (licenses clinical social worker associate) After you pay me directly, I will provide you with a receipt that you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement,

Private pay- This means that you would be required to pay upfront. I will request that you have a card on file so that I can omit the hassle of collecting payment after our session is completed. I will automatically charge your card upon completion of the session.
